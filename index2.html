Cowboy Bebop

Cowboy Bebop is a Japanese science fiction neo noir anime series. The series premired in Japan on TV Tokyo from April 3 to June 26 in 1998.

  • Due to it's controversial adult themed content, at the time, only tweleve episodes were aired and instead the entire 26 episodes were broadcasted on Wowow.
  • Since it's release, ctritics have hailed Cowboy Bebop as one of the greatest animated television series of all time.
